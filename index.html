Electoral Mandate

The victorious party in this election will assume complete administrative control over the Fourth Supreme Republic of Rasputin, encompassing all 54 states and 18 republics. This mandate includes:

  • Executive authority over national policy and governance
  • Strategic control of military and defense operations
  • Direction of international diplomatic relations
  • Management of national resources and economic policy
Electoral Race System:

The bars represent each party's progress toward victory:

  • First party to achieve 5 filled bars wins complete control of the Fourth Supreme Republic of Rasputin
  • Each bar represents major electoral victories in key regions
